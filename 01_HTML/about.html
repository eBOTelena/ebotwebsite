HOW TO USE THE APPLICATIONS

DRAWING PAD If you love digital drawing this is the application for you! While you are drawing in the screen, όrasis will track your eye movements at the same time. Lets see who can draw better.

SNAKE GAME Did you use to play the snake game as well? This was my favorite childhood game. Just use the keyboard arrows to control the snake while όrasis will follow your eyes and draw on paper. Lets see where your snake is going.

These applications are only some suggestions of how you can use the drawing machine. You are the user and therefore, can use it in any way you like.

ΣTORY


The name "όrasis", derives from the greek word "όραση", which means eyesight.
Nobody knows the future with certainty but there is no question about technology being in it. Being passionate to explore and imagine objects and situations in an innovative way with an aim of technology enhancing our life has led to this development. Through this project, the aim was to have a real sense of playfulness and uniqueness that connects people and their capabilities with technology and the future. This idea of inviting the audience to engage with something that might not be that familiar in their everyday life but will somehow make a change is what motivates me in my studio work.
